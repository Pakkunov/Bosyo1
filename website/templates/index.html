Who are we?

Bosyo Garbage Collector is your local Residential Waste removal service for subdivisions.

We are a local company serving areas within Bacoor, Cavite. The business first started in the year 2005 and has ever been growing successfully. We are here to provide an effective, dependable, and outstanding service to our clients.

We take almost all types of household garbage items. Here are some of the examples of the items we take:

Care for the environment.

At Bosyo Garbage Collector, we are dedicated to serving you and saving our environment. After each collection from subdivisions, we directly dispose of them to our landfills. We carefully segregate our recyclables and general waste properly to protect everyone's health.
