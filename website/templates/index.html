FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account? To create an account, please contact us directly. We only work with neighborhoods and HOA's to provide excellent pricing for organized residential areas. We'll need to get in touch with you to gather some important information, such as your contact details and the location. This will help us to set up your account and provide you with accurate pricing information based on your specific needs.
What areas do you service? As a trusted provider of waste management solutions, we offer efficient and reliable garbage collection and disposal services to a wide range of cities and communities around Bacoor, Cavite.
Do you accept cash payments for your service? We accept payments via credit card and PayPal, but if you prefer an alternative, we also accept payment via check.

Billing will be sent to the central organization (HOA). Our clients will be able to login online via our website. We believe in clear and transparent pricing, so you always know exactly what you're paying for. If you have any billing questions, our team is always available to help.
Where should I place my garbage? In order to ensure that our waste management crew can efficiently and safely collect your trash, we kindly request that you make sure your trash can is visible and unobstructed at all times. This means that your trash can should be positioned in a location that is easily seen and easily accessible without any obstacles or barriers in the way.

By doing so, you'll help us maintain a timely and reliable waste management service for you and your community.
Can I still use your services if I'm not a member of an HOA? Our services are exclusively available for Homeowners Association (HOA) members. This is because we work closely with HOAs to provide comprehensive waste management solutions tailored to their specific needs.

If you're not currently a member of an HOA, we recommend contacting your local community association to inquire about joining.
What if I need to reschedule a collection? If you need to reschedule your garbage collection, please contact us as soon as possible so that we can make the necessary arrangements. We understand that unexpected events can occur, and we strive to be as flexible as possible to accommodate our customers' needs.
What measures do you take to ensure environmentally responsible waste management? We care about the environment and follow responsible waste management practices.

We properly sort and dispose of waste according to local regulations, and promote recycling to reduce waste. In addition, we have our own landfill to ensure proper disposal of garbage.
Do you accept construction debris? We only accept household waste. Please be advised that we are unable to accept any kind of construction debris, such as sheetrock, shingles, tile, marble, concrete, and other similar materials.
